Photography

Principe History Art

Photography, in spite of its long history, has only recently become established. Indeed, this one is available to the large public only recently. This accessibility is made thanks to the inlay of photographic system in our phones.

The democratization of Photography naturally brought about contests. Competitions brought evolutions and perfection. This perfection thus led us to the art of Photography.

Nowadays, many competitions, such as the EyeEm Photography Awards, are held every year to showcase the existing talent in this art.
